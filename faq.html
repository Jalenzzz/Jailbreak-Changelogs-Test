Jailbreak is a 12-time award-winning game where you can orchestrate a robbery or catch criminals! Team up with friends for even more fun and plan the ultimate police raid or criminal heist. It can be played here and is made by Badimo.

Badimo are the creators of the hit game Jailbreak.

The website was created to provide a central hub for all Jailbreak Changelog updates, reward details, and season information. It's a convenient place to stay informed about the latest changes and explore the evolution of Jailbreak over time.

You can stay updated by visiting the Jailbreak Changelogs homepage, where the latest season and updates are showcased. The timeline section provides a comprehensive history of all past and present changelogs.

While the website is dedicated to changelogs, we encourage you to join the Jailbreak community on platforms like Discord to engage with other players, share ideas, and discuss updates. Links to official community platforms are available through the website.

Feedback is always welcome! You can share your thoughts by using #suggestions or by contributing directly to the project on GitHub.

If you need assistance with the website, you can reach out to v3kmw or jalenzz16.

The website is open source! You can contribute by visiting the GitHub repository and submitting your pull requests after making improvements or changes.

Changelogs are sourced from the Jailbreak Wiki, the official Discord changelogs channel, Twitter posts by Badimo, asimo3089, and badcc, as well as community contributions.

Jailbreak is available on Roblox, which can be played on PC, mobile devices, and consoles.

Yes, Jailbreak offers in-game purchases for items and upgrades, but the game can be enjoyed without spending money.

Badimo announced on X that the monthly update cycle will be discontinued after the live event somewhere in late-April 2024.

Players can report bugs through the official Discord server or by using this form: Report Form.

Players can follow Badimo, asimo3089, and badcc on X, join the game's official Discord at discord.gg/jailbreak, or participate in the Reddit community at r/robloxjailbreak.

Release dates are recorded in the U.S. (EST/EDT) time zone. Some dates may be inaccurate due to differing time zones like (CST/CDT) which is one hour behind eastern.
